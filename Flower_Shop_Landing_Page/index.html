VARIETIES

Blue Poppy

The blue poppy, known for its strikingly vibrant blue petals, symbolizes rare beauty and the exotic allure of the Himalayan landscapes where it originates.

Red Spider Lily

The red spider lily, with its striking scarlet petals and delicate, spidery filaments, symbolizes both passionate love and the poignant beauty of fleeting moments.

Aconitum Violaceum

Aconitum violaceum, also known as the violet monkshood, is a strikingly beautiful yet highly toxic flower native to the mountainous regions of Asia, known for its vibrant purple petals and distinctive hood-like shape.

Erigeron Multiradiatus

Erigeron multiradiatus, also known as Himalayan fleabane, is a vibrant perennial wildflower celebrated for its daisy-like, lavender-pink blooms and its resilience in alpine environments.

Geranium Robertianum

Geranium Robertianum, commonly known as Herb Robert, is a charming, resilient wildflower known for its bright pink flowers and distinctive medicinal properties.

Allium Humile

Allium humile, a delicate and charming flowering plant, is known for its elegant, star-shaped blossoms and subtle fragrance, making it a beautiful addition to any garden.
